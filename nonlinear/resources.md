# Resources: Non-Linear Methods & Clustering

## Key Papers

### UMAP
- McInnes, Healy & Melville (2018). [UMAP: Uniform Manifold Approximation and Projection for Dimension Reduction](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.03426). --- The original UMAP paper.

### CEBRA
- Schneider, Lee & Bhagavatula (2023). [Learnable latent embeddings for joint behavioural and neural analysis](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06031-6). Nature. --- Introduces CEBRA for consistent neural embeddings.

## Tutorials and Explanations

### UMAP
- [How UMAP Works](https://umap-learn.readthedocs.io/en/latest/how_umap_works.html) --- Official documentation explaining the algorithm in detail
- [Understanding UMAP (Google PAIR)](https://pair-code.github.io/understanding-umap/) --- Interactive visualization of UMAP behavior with different parameters. Highly recommended.
- [StatQuest: UMAP](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eN0wFzBA4Sc) --- Accessible video explanation

### CEBRA
- [CEBRA Documentation](https://cebra.ai/) --- Official docs with tutorials and examples
- [CEBRA GitHub](https://github.com/AdaptiveMotorControlLab/CEBRA) --- Source code and notebook examples

## Clustering
- [scikit-learn: Clustering](https://scikit-learn.org/stable/modules/clustering.html) --- K-Means, DBSCAN, and many other clustering algorithms with examples
- [Visualizing DBSCAN Clustering](https://www.naftaliharris.com/blog/visualizing-dbscan-clustering/) --- Interactive visualization of DBSCAN

## Software

- [umap-learn](https://umap-learn.readthedocs.io/) --- Official UMAP Python package
- [openTSNE](https://opentsne.readthedocs.io/) --- Fast, extensible t-SNE (for comparison)
- [scikit-learn](https://scikit-learn.org/) --- K-Means, DBSCAN, and more
