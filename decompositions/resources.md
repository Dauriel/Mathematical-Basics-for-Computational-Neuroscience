# Resources: Eigentheory & Decompositions

## Textbooks

- **Gilbert Strang, *Linear Algebra and Its Applications*** --- Chapters on eigenvalues, SVD, and positive definite matrices
- **Steven Strogatz, *Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos*** --- The classic text on dynamical systems, stability analysis, and phase portraits. Essential companion for the dynamical systems section.

## Online Courses and Lectures

- [MIT OCW 18.06SC: Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06sc-linear-algebra-fall-2011/) --- Strang's lectures 21--22 on eigenvalues, and the SVD lecture
- [3Blue1Brown: Eigenvectors and Eigenvalues](https://www.3blue1brown.com/topics/linear-algebra) --- Visual explanation of what eigenvalues and eigenvectors mean geometrically

## MIT OCW PDFs

- [SVD Summary (MIT 18.06SC)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06sc-linear-algebra-fall-2011/d273f75ee2552a5c3c35ccab37e5edce_MIT18_06SCF11_Ses3.5sum.pdf) --- Concise summary of SVD derivation and properties
- [Complex Matrices and Fast Fourier Transform (MIT 18.06SC)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06sc-linear-algebra-fall-2011/53f17d47bf99d26fdc472b3177c256a8_MIT18_06SCF11_Ses3.2sum.pdf) --- Summary of complex vector spaces and the FFT

## Dynamical Systems

- [MIT OCW 18.03SC: Differential Equations](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-03sc-differential-equations-fall-2011/) --- For more depth on linear systems and phase portraits
- [Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos (Strogatz lectures on YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbN57C5Zdl6j_qJA-pARJnKsmROzPnO9V) --- Full lecture series by Strogatz
